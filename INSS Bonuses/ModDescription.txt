Adds a really simple, INSS bonus system to any map. 
Players will get income based on the amount of territories they own of a bonus and the bonus value. The more territories you have in a bonus, the more income you get

Imagine a bonus which contains out of 5 territories and with the value 3, then you'll get the following income
1 territory = -1
2 territories = 0
3 territories = 1
4 territories = 2
5 territories = 3
