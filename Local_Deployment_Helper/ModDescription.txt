Credits to TBest who came up with this solution!
 - takes your order from the previous turn and adds them to your order list
 - Automatic local deployment bonus overwriter
 - Recommended to play with 'can attack with percentage'!
Few notes to take into account:
 - It can occur players are not able to commit their orders because the helper function deployed to many armies with the following settings:
   - army cap
   - sanction cards
   - army multiplier (commerce games only)
   - negative bonuses (the automatic bonus overwriter overwrites these to 0)
